Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you use our app:

  • Account Information: If you log in with Google, we collect your email address and profile picture for authentication purposes.
  • User Content: We store the tasks (todos), project data, and settings you create within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provides the service:

  • To authenticate your identity and sync your data across devices.
  • To maintain and improve the app's functionality.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud).

4. Data Deletion

You can request data deletion by deleting your account within the app settings or contacting us.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us via the developer contact information on the Google Play Store.
